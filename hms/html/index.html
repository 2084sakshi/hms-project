Welcome to Our Hospital Management System

Efficiently managing healthcare services through our advanced system. Simplifying appointments, medical histories, and more for a seamless experience.

About the System

Our Hospital Management System is designed to streamline day-to-day operations, from booking appointments to managing patient records. This system brings together patients and doctors, providing a user-friendly interface to enhance healthcare services.

Powered by React.js (Frontend), Node.js, and Express (Backend), with MySQL as the database, our system ensures secure and efficient data management.

Patient Features

  • Book appointments with preferred doctors.
  • Provide and access previous medical history.
  • View, update, or cancel booked appointments.
  • Ensure privacy with a secure system.
  • Access complete diagnosis, prescriptions, and medical history.

Doctor Features

  • Manage schedules and appointments efficiently.
  • Access patient history and profiles.
  • Provide and modify diagnoses and prescriptions.
  • Ensure a clash-free appointment system.
